About Kamalia Khaddar

Preserving the art of handcrafted textiles since 1985. Authentic Khaddar, woven with tradition, designed for modern elegance.

Our Story

Kamalia, a historic city in the Toba Tek Singh district of Punjab, Pakistan, has been renowned for its exquisite Khaddar fabric for centuries. Our journey began in 1985 when our founders decided to bring the finest handwoven textiles directly to customers.

Every piece is crafted by skilled artisans using traditional handloom techniques passed down through generations. We take immense pride in preserving this heritage while adapting to contemporary fashion sensibilities.

Today, Kamalia Khaddar serves customers nationwide, offering premium suits, unstitched fabrics, and a curated collection of cotton, silk, and linen textiles — all at accessible prices.
